# Sparkwavv User Guide: Document Outline

This document outlines the structure and key topics for the upcoming **Sparkwavv User Guide**. The guide is designed to help users navigate the Sparkwavv application and successfully complete the 12-week career transformation journey.

---

## 1. Introduction
*   **Welcome to Sparkwavv:** Overview of the mission and platform.
*   **The 12-Week Journey Philosophy:** Understanding the systemic transition through five critical phases.
*   **Meet Skylar:** Introduction to your AI Career Architect and how to interact with "her".

## 2. Getting Started
*   **Account Setup & Registration:** First steps to joining the platform.
*   **Initializing Your Wavvault:** How to start your digital career repository.
*   **Dashboard Overview:** Navigating the "Operations Control" and user dashboard.

## 3. Phase 1: Dive-In (Weeks 1-2)
*   **Objective:** Foundation & Identity.
*   **Key Activities:**
    *   Defining your Professional Identity.
    *   Identifying Core Strengths.
    *   Sharing Initial Career Stories.
*   **The "Spark" Identification:** Finding your core motivation.
*   **Validation Gate 1:** Requirements for moving to Ignition (Commitment & Spark).

## 4. Phase 2: Ignition (Weeks 3-4)
*   **Objective:** Momentum & Clarity.
*   **Key Activities:**
    *   Deepening Career Narratives.
    *   Initial Market Research & Alignment.
*   **Validation Gate 2:** The "Five Stories" Validation and RPP (Real Professional Person) feedback.

## 5. Phase 3: Discovery (Weeks 5-6)
*   **Objective:** Exploration & Insights.
*   **Key Activities:**
    *   Informational Interviewing strategies.
    *   Skill Gap Analysis.
*   **Tools:** Using the **Discovery Bento** and **Skylar Side Panel** for research.

## 6. Phase 4: Branding (Weeks 7-9)
*   **Objective:** Positioning & Presence.
*   **Key Activities:**
    *   Resume & LinkedIn Optimization.
    *   Defining your Personal Brand DNA.
*   **Tools:** Introduction to the **Outreach Forge**.

## 7. Phase 5: Outreach (Weeks 10-12)
*   **Objective:** Execution & Landing.
*   **Key Activities:**
    *   Active Networking & Strategic Outreach.
    *   Interview Preparation with AI simulations.
    *   Job Matching & Application tracking.

## 8. Advanced Features & AI Tools
*   **Neural Synthesis Engine:** How Sparkwavv synthesizes your data into insights.
*   **High-Fidelity Synthesis Lab:** Advanced career modeling.
*   **The Wavvault:** Managing your long-term digital career repository.

## 9. Support & Troubleshooting
*   **Help Center:** Accessing documentation and FAQs.
*   **Technical Support:** What to do when things go wrong.
*   **Community & Mentorship:** Connecting with others on the journey.
