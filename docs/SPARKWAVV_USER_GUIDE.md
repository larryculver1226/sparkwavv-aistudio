## 1. Introduction

### 1.1 Platform Philosophy
Sparkwavv is a systemic career transformation platform designed to move candidates from professional stagnation to high-alignment roles. The journey is structured into five distinct phases, each separated by a **Validation Gate** to ensure data integrity and psychological readiness.

### 1.2 Meet Skylar
Skylar is your AI Career Architect. She is not a simple chatbot; she is a sophisticated synthesis engine that analyzes your "Wavvault" data to identify patterns, strengths, and market opportunities.

> **Skylar says:** *"I’m here to help you find the signal in the noise. My job is to synthesize your history into a future that actually fits. Let's get started."*

## 3. Phase 1: Dive-In (Weeks 1-2)

### 3.1 Objective: Foundation & Identity
The goal of the Dive-In phase is to establish a baseline of your professional identity and identify your initial "Spark"—the core motivation driving your transition.

### 3.2 Key Activities
*   **Identity Mapping:** Describing your professional self in a raw, unfiltered narrative.
*   **Strength Identification:** Cataloging technical and soft skills.
*   **Story Collection:** Documenting formative career experiences.

### 3.3 Skylar Tool: Wavvault Initializer
Skylar uses the data provided in this phase to build your initial **Neural Synthesis**.

> **[Visual Placeholder: Dive-In UX Diagram]**
> *Description: Mermaid diagram showing the flow from Auth -> Welcome -> Onboarding Steps -> Skylar Initialization -> Validation Gate.*

### 3.4 Validation Gate 1: Commitment & Spark
To move to Phase 2, you must pass the first Validation Gate.
*   **Requirements:** 100% completion of Onboarding; identification of a viable "Spark".
*   **The Gap:** If the Spark is too vague (e.g., "I want a better job"), Skylar will block progression and prompt for deeper reflection on specific values and interests.

---

## 4. Phase 2: Ignition (Weeks 3-4)

### 4.1 Objective: Momentum & Clarity
Ignition focuses on turning your raw history into structured narratives that resonate with the market.

### 4.2 Key Activities
*   **The Five Stories:** Developing five core professional narratives that demonstrate your unique value.
*   **Market Alignment:** Initial mapping of your Spark to specific industries or roles.

### 4.3 Validation Gate 2: RPP Validation
*   **Requirements:** Your "Five Stories" must be reviewed and validated by a **Real Professional Person (RPP)**—typically a Mentor or Partner.
*   **The Gap:** "Solitary Confinement Bias." If you attempt to proceed without objective feedback, Skylar will issue a **Hard Block** or **Soft Warning** depending on your tenant settings.

---

## 5. Phase 3: Discovery (Weeks 5-6)

### 5.1 Objective: Exploration & Insights
Discovery is about testing your hypotheses in the real world through informational interviewing and skill gap analysis.

### 5.2 Skylar Tool: Discovery Bento
A modular interface that helps you track research on companies, roles, and industry trends.

### 5.3 Validation Gate 3: Market Resonance
*   **Requirements:** Completion of at least three informational interviews; documented skill gap analysis.
*   **The Gap:** Lack of external data. If your Wavvault only contains internal reflections, you cannot move to the Branding phase.

---

## 6. Phase 4: Branding (Weeks 7-9)

### 6.1 Objective: Positioning & Presence
In this phase, you translate your Discovery insights into a high-impact professional brand.

### 6.2 Key Activities
*   **Resume/LinkedIn Optimization:** Tailoring your digital presence to your target roles.
*   **Brand DNA:** Defining the core attributes that make you a "Category of One".

### 6.4 Validation Gate 4: Brand Integrity
*   **Requirements:** Finalized Resume/LinkedIn; Brand DNA synthesis approved by Skylar.
*   **The Gap:** Brand Mismatch. If your branding doesn't align with the Discovery data, Skylar will flag inconsistencies for review.

---

## 7. Phase 5: Outreach (Weeks 10-12)

### 7.1 Objective: Execution & Landing
The final phase is the active pursuit of your target roles.

### 7.2 Skylar Tool: Outreach Forge
A CRM-style interface for managing networking contacts, application statuses, and interview schedules.

### 7.3 Final Gate: Transition
*   **Requirements:** Active interview pipeline; final synthesis of the 12-week journey.
*   **Outcome:** Successful landing in a role that aligns with your Spark.

## 9. Partner & Mentor Integration

### 9.1 The Mentor's Role
Mentors provide the objective "RPP" validation required at key gates. They have access to a **Partner Dashboard** where they can review your progress and provide feedback.

### 9.2 Documentation for Partners
Partners should refer to the **Partner Operational Framework** (available in the Help Center) for detailed instructions on conducting Gate Reviews.
